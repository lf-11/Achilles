MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT ("the MSA") — dated 14 March 2024
between Meridian Retail Group plc ("the Customer") and TechFlow Solutions Limited ("the Supplier")

Clause 1.2 — The Supplier shall perform the Services with reasonable skill and care.

Clause 3.1 [P13] — The Supplier shall use reasonable endeavours to achieve the target go-live
date set out in the Statement of Work. The implementation timetable is a planning estimate and
may be adjusted through the change control procedure.

Clause 3.2 [P14] — Any change to the scope, deliverables or timetable shall be agreed in writing
by both parties by means of a signed Change Order. A signed Change Order varies this Agreement to
the extent stated in it.

Clause 14.1 [P16] — Subject to clause 14.3, the Supplier shall not be liable, whether in contract,
tort (including negligence) or otherwise, for any loss of profit, loss of revenue or loss of
anticipated savings.

Clause 14.2 [P17] — The Supplier's total aggregate liability under or in connection with this
Agreement shall not exceed the total charges actually paid by the Customer under this Agreement.

Clause 22.1 [P20] — This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and
supersedes all prior representations, arrangements, understandings and agreements between them,
whether written or oral.

Clause 22.2 [P21] — The Customer acknowledges that it has not relied on, and shall have no remedy
in respect of, any statement, representation, assurance or warranty (whether made innocently or
negligently) that is not expressly set out in this Agreement (save in the case of fraud).
