[P18] The wasted expenditure of £1,800,000 is consistent with the Claimant's accounting records and
I have no reason to challenge this element.

[P20] In my opinion the pleaded loss of profit of £4,200,000 is substantially overstated.

[P21] A supportable figure for Platform-attributable loss of profit is of the order of £1,300,000,
not £4,200,000.

[P22] Approximately £1,500,000 of the claimed decline is attributable to these non-Platform factors
(the Lutterworth distribution-centre flood and a wider market downturn, the latter evident in the
data before go-live); the balance is unsupported.
