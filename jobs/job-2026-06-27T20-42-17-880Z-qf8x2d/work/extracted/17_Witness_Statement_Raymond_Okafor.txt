WITNESS STATEMENT OF RAYMOND OKAFOR
(Head of Retail Operations, Meridian Retail Group plc) — dated 13 March 2026

[P15] After go-live I saw the impact in stores first hand, with stock figures being wrong at the
tills on the new system. The new platform went live on 18 November 2024, immediately before our
peak trading period.

[P16] There was a complete outage on 25 November that I am told was a network failure at our data
centre provider, not the platform. Some of the till outages in stores were caused by the broadband
connections in the stores dropping out, rather than the platform itself.

[P17] Our distribution centre at Lutterworth flooded on 3 December 2024, which badly disrupted
fulfilment. The market was also weak that quarter.
