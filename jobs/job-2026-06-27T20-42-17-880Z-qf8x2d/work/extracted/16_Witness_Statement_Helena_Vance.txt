WITNESS STATEMENT OF HELENA VANCE
(Programme Director, Meridian Retail Group plc) — dated 13 March 2026

I had overall responsibility for the Platform implementation programme on Meridian's side.

[P16] Around August 2024 the board decided it wanted a customer loyalty capability. I asked TechFlow
to add a loyalty module. I accept this was not in the original Statement of Work. It was documented in
Change Order No. 3, which my colleague Priya Nair signed on 2 September 2024, and which also moved the
go-live date to 18 November 2024.

[P17] TechFlow recommended that we defer go-live. I took the decision to proceed despite TechFlow's
advice, because we needed to be live for peak trading.

[P18] The stock-synchronisation problems after go-live caused a great deal of disruption for store
teams during our busiest period.
