LETTER — TECHFLOW'S SOLICITORS' RESPONSE — 7 February 2025
From Hartwell & Pryce LLP (for TechFlow Solutions Limited) to Meridian Retail Group plc

The go-live date was a target, not a fixed date. It moved from 1 October to 18 November 2024 by
reason of Change Order No. 3, signed by your client on 2 September 2024, which added a customer
loyalty module that your client requested and which was not in the original scope. Your client's
assertion that it requested no change to scope is contradicted by its own signed Change Order.

Your client was advised in writing to defer go-live and chose to proceed and to accept the risk.

Your client accepted Phase 1 by a signed Acceptance Certificate dated 12 November 2024.

The Platform was not unavailable for anything approaching 40% of trading hours; the largest outage
(25 November 2024) was caused by your client's own network provider, not the Platform.

The pleaded loss of profit is substantially overstated and is in any event excluded and capped by
clause 14 of the MSA.

Your client has wrongly withheld the final two milestone payments totalling £600,000, which remain
due to our client.
